Whether you're paying international suppliers, disbursing funds to remote workers, or settling cross-border invoices, RemitFlex gives you a single REST API to pay, convert, and off-ramp stablecoins seamlessly — backed by compliant infrastructure that handles the complexity of international corridors for you.

## Why RemitFlex

RemitFlex removes the friction that makes cross-border payments slow and expensive. You send stablecoins; your recipients receive local currency in their bank accounts. RemitFlex handles the conversion, routing, compliance checks, and settlement in between.

## How Onboarding Works

Getting your first payment live takes three steps. You can complete the entire flow in an afternoon.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up at [dashboard.remitflex.com](https://dashboard.remitflex.com) and complete identity verification for your business. Once approved, your account is provisioned with sandbox access immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate the API">
    Grab your test API key from the dashboard, follow the [Quickstart](/quickstart) to make your first call, and build your integration against the sandbox environment — no real funds involved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go Live">
    Switch to your live API key, fund your RemitFlex wallet with stablecoins, and start sending real payments. The [API Reference](/api-reference/overview) has everything you need to handle production edge cases.
  </Step>
</Steps>
